Bangkok

Bangkok is a city of contrasts. Smells (good and bad) assault the senses, traffic buzzes all around and vendors jostle for attention at every turn. On the other hand, it’s a city with a serene soul - full of imperial temples and beaming smiles from the world’s friendliest people.
